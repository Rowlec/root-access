# workflow-spec.md

## Startup Proposal Workflow

### Step 1 — Problem Discovery

Goal:
Identify a real pain point.

Tool:
ChatGPT

Output:

* pain point
* why it matters
* who experiences it

---

### Step 2 — Target Customer

Goal:
Define the exact target user.

Tool:
ChatGPT

Output:

* age
* behavior
* needs
* frustrations

---

### Step 3 — Existing Solutions

Goal:
Research competitors.

Tool:
ChatGPT + Google

Output:

* direct competitors
* indirect competitors
* weaknesses

---

### Step 4 — Value Proposition

Goal:
Define what makes the idea different.

Tool:
ChatGPT

Output:

* USP
* why now
* why better

---

### Step 5 — Business Model

Goal:
Generate revenue model.

Tool:
ChatGPT

Output:

* pricing
* revenue source
* scalability

---

### Step 6 — MVP Design

Goal:
Define smallest version.

Tool:
ChatGPT

Output:

* MVP scope
* key features
* excluded features

---

### Step 7 — Pitch Structure

Goal:
Create pitch deck structure.

Tool:
Gamma

Output:

* slide structure
* storytelling flow
